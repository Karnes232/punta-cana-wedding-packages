# Website Structure

## Punta Cana Wedding Packages

What the website is, what each page does, and how everything connects to the sales funnel. This is the business blueprint, not the technical blueprint.

---

## General Purpose

Punta Cana Wedding Packages is **not a promotional brochure.** It is a **structured sales system** where visitors:

1. **Discover** (via Home or blog articles)
2. **Understand** (via How It Works, About Us, Contact)
3. **Build** (via Wedding Calculator—the core)
4. **Lead** (via form submission)
5. **Follow up** (team contacts directly; no payment on site)

The website's main goal is to **move qualified visitors toward the Wedding Calculator and form submission.** Every other page exists to support that conversion.

## Page Details

### Home Page (`/:locale/`)

**Exact Purpose:**
Introduce the brand and platform clearly. First impression. A new visitor should immediately understand that this is a place where they can design their wedding and get an instant estimate.

**What It Should NOT Do:**
❌ Explain everything in heavy detail
❌ Overwhelm with features
❌ Use jargon or complexity

**Recommended Section Order:**

1. **Hero Section**
   - Clear headline: "Design Your Dream Wedding in Minutes"
   - Supporting message: "Build your perfect Punta Cana wedding and get an instant estimate"
   - Strong primary button: "Start Designing My Wedding" (→ calculator)
   - Optional: Hero image showing beautiful wedding setting

2. **How It Works (Quick Version)**
   - 3–4 steps: Design → Estimate → Plan → Book
   - Brief explanation without requiring separate page visit
   - Builds confidence immediately

3. **Wedding Builder Introduction**
   - What is the calculator?
   - How does it work?
   - Why use it?
   - CTA: "Start Building"

4. **What Can Be Customized**
   - Show flexibility: "Menu, drinks, decor, photography, extras..."
   - Reassure that every wedding is unique
   - Visual list of categories

5. **Transparent Pricing Section**
   - Explain that estimates are predetermined, not manual
   - Range examples (e.g., "weddings typically range from $X to $Y")
   - CTA: "See Your Estimate"

6. **Booking Process Overview**
   - "Submit form → Team responds → Secure date with deposit → Plan together"
   - Simple, reassuring flow

7. **Why Choose Us**
   - Company credentials, experience, location
   - Sanity venue (Cabeza de Toro)
   - Professional coordination
   - 24/7 support

8. **Strong Call-to-Action Section**
   - "Ready to plan?" with prominent button
   - Secondary CTA: "View Stories" (social proof)

9. **Simple Lead Form or Contact Strip**
   - Optional: Quick "get more info" form
   - Or just "Questions? Contact us" with WhatsApp/email

10. **Footer**
    - Logo, contact info (email, WhatsApp)
    - Quick links: Blog, Stories, How It Works, About, Contact
    - Legal links: Privacy, Terms
    - Social links (if applicable)

**Main Objective:** Conversion through clarity. Visitor should feel comfortable clicking calculator.

### Wedding Calculator (`/:locale/wedding-calculator`)

**Exact Purpose:**
This is the **core of the entire platform.** The most important page.

Allow the user to build the wedding step by step. Should feel elegant, intuitive, highly guided—NOT like a spreadsheet or back-office system.

User should:

- Select date, guests, hotel, venue, menu, drinks, decor, photo, video, music, transport, extras
- See estimated total update live
- Never feel overwhelmed
- Feel that the platform is helping them create something beautiful

**Experience:**

- Guided wizard (one step at a time)
- Live price updates
- Visual, aspirational (not technical)
- Clear summaries
- Easy-to-use form submission

See `wedding-builder-logic.md` for detailed calculation rules and step-by-step flow.

**Main Objective:** Submission of complete wedding configuration with estimated total.

---

### How It Works Page (`/:locale/how-it-works`)

**Exact Purpose:**
Explain the customer journey in more detail than the quick version on Home. Help visitors understand the entire process is simple, structured, and professional.

**Recommended Sections:**

1. **Hero**
   - "Our Simple Wedding Planning Process"
   - "Build your wedding in minutes, work with our team for perfection"

2. **The Configuration Process**
   - Explain that users select: guests, menu, drinks, decor, photo, video, transport, extras
   - Visual: Step icons or illustrations

3. **Automatic Price Calculation**
   - Explain in plain language that the system calculates based on predetermined pricing
   - Reassure that estimates are accurate and professional
   - "No surprises, no manual quotes"

4. **Form Submission**
   - What happens when you submit?
   - Full wedding config is sent to planning team by email
   - Team receives everything organized and ready

5. **The Team Takes Over**
   - Personal wedding assistant assigned
   - Direct communication (email, WhatsApp, phone)
   - Available 24/7 for questions
   - Venue visit available (if couple wants to visit)

6. **Booking & Payment Process** ⭐ IMPORTANT

   **Required Deposit:**
   - "To secure your wedding date, a **US$500 deposit** is required"
   - "This deposit is fully deductible from your total wedding cost"
   - "Deposit is paid directly to our team (not on the website)"

   **Timeline:**
   - "Submit form today"
   - "Our team responds within 24 hours"
   - "Discuss and confirm details"
   - "Pay US$500 to secure date"

   **Payment Schedule:**
   - "30 days before wedding: Pay 50% of total"
   - "15 days before wedding: Pay remaining 50%"
   - "Wedding must be fully paid 15 days before event"

   **Flexibility:**
   - "Need to move your date? No problem!"
   - "Date can be changed without extra fee (if new date available)"
   - "This is one of our key strengths for international couples"

   **Available Now, Pay Later:**
   - "You can secure a date years in advance"
   - "Example: Reserve now for December 2028 wedding"
   - "Perfect for couples planning ahead"

7. **Support & Availability**
   - 24/7 availability
   - Personal assistant
   - Flexible planning
   - Responsive communication

8. **FAQ Section** (if helpful)
   - Common questions about process, payment, dates, changes

9. **CTA at Bottom**
   - "Ready to start?" → Button to calculator
   - "Have questions?" → Button to contact page

### About Us Page (`/:locale/about`)

**Exact Purpose:**
Build emotional trust. Show that there's a real, capable team behind the platform.

**What It Should Cover:**

1. **Company Story**
   - How the company started
   - Why destination weddings (Punta Cana specifically)
   - Evolution toward platform/calculator

2. **Venue & Location**
   - Cabeza de Toro location
   - Why it's special (beach, location, flexibility, etc.)
   - Capacity, amenities, flexibility

3. **Team**
   - Key team members (photos + brief bios)
   - Expertise in destination weddings
   - Credentials, years of experience
   - Personal touch (faces, names, stories)

4. **What Makes Them Different**
   - Specialization in destination weddings
   - Can manage multiple wedding elements in one place
   - Easier for international couples
   - Personal touch + professional system

5. **Values**
   - Clarity (transparent pricing)
   - Simplicity (easy process)
   - Elegance (beautiful weddings)
   - Support (24/7 availability)

6. **CTA**
   - "Let's plan your wedding" → Calculator
   - "Questions?" → Contact page

**Emotional Goal:** Visitor moves from interest into trust, ready to use calculator.

### Contact Page (`/:locale/contact`)

**Exact Purpose:**
Simple, welcoming direct communication. People who aren't ready to use calculator yet can ask questions.

**Elements:**

1. **Contact Form**
   - Name, email, message (simple)
   - Optional: Phone, WhatsApp
   - Friendly, not sales-y
   - Thank you confirmation

2. **Direct Contact Info**
   - **Email:** [support email]
   - **WhatsApp:** [team WhatsApp number]
   - **Phone:** [if available]
   - **Location:** Punta Cana, Dominican Republic

3. **Map** (optional)
   - Sanity location or Google Map
   - Visual reference to Punta Cana

4. **Response Time**
   - "We typically respond within 24 hours"
   - Builds expectation of responsiveness

5. **Alternative CTAs**
   - "Ready to build?" → Calculator
   - "Want to see examples?" → Stories

**Feeling:** Reachable, responsive, friendly, not pushy.

### Blog (`/:locale/blog`)

**Strategic Importance:**
The blog is critical for long-term traffic and lead generation. It is **not optional or secondary.** It is part of the conversion funnel.

**Purpose:**
Attract people who are searching for answers about destination weddings, Punta Cana costs, planning advice, etc. Many aren't ready to book immediately, but they are potential future clients.

**Structure:**

1. **Blog Index Page**
   - List of all articles
   - Possibly organized by category
   - Featured/popular articles
   - Search (if helpful)

2. **Individual Article Pages**
   - Full article content
   - Author, publish date
   - Related articles
   - Internal CTAs: "Ready to plan?" → Calculator

**Content Direction:**

- How much does a wedding in Punta Cana cost?
- Wedding budget guidance
- What's included in destination weddings?
- Best times of year to marry
- Small vs. large wedding decisions
- Guest transportation logistics
- Menu planning tips
- Open bar costs
- Real wedding inspiration
- Decor ideas
- Timeline recommendations
- Wedding planning checklists
- How far in advance to book?
- Cost per guest estimation
- Welcome dinner ideas
- Farewell brunch ideas
- Wedding weekend planning

**Blog-to-Conversion Connection:**
Each article should include internal CTAs that guide readers toward calculator or inquiry. The flow is:

→ User discovers blog via search
→ Reads helpful content
→ Builds trust in brand
→ Clicks "Calculate Your Wedding" or "See How the Builder Works"
→ Uses calculator
→ Submits form

**Blog is NOT separate from the funnel.** It is the top of the funnel.

### Stories / Testimonials (`/:locale/stories`)

**Exact Purpose:**
Real wedding inspiration and social proof. Show beautiful, real weddings that have been planned via this platform.

**Structure:**

1. **Stories Index**
   - Gallery or list of weddings
   - Filter by proposal type? (Classic, Modern, Dining, etc.)
   - Preview cards with image, couple name, brief description

2. **Individual Story Page**
   - Full story of the wedding
   - Multiple photos
   - Couple's testimonial
   - Wedding details (guests, menu, theme, highlights)
   - CTA: "Plan a wedding like this" → Calculator

**Emotional Goal:** Visitors see real examples, feel inspired, build confidence that beautiful weddings are achievable here.

## Conversion Goals (In Order of Importance)

1. **Wedding Calculator Submission** (primary)
   - User completes full config
   - Form submitted
   - Email sent to team
   - **Success:** Qualified lead received

2. **Contact Form Inquiry** (secondary)
   - User has questions before using calculator
   - Direct communication channel
   - **Success:** Engaged potential lead

3. **Blog Discovery** (long-term)
   - Organic search visibility
   - Content-driven leads
   - **Success:** Future calculator users

---

## Important Notes for Team

- **Calculator is the heart.** Everything else supports it.
- **Transparency is key.** Always explain pricing, process, payment upfront.
- **Design should feel calm, not pushy.** Avoid aggressive sales language.
- **Blog is long-term investment.** Not immediate ROI but critical for sustainability.
- **Personal touch matters.** Show team, real couples, real stories.
- **Mobile-first.** Many users browse on phones in travel planning mode.
